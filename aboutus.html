[ How the Company Started ]

Bean Around the Block was founded in 2015 by our beloved Joe Bean. Having worked as a
construction labourer for 10 years Joe had been getting tired of wasting the little break time he
was given, standing in line to get his daily coffee which wasnt that good. There just had to be a
better and faster way to get coffee without wasting both personal and company time.

 

And so began the idea for Bean Around the Block. Joe wanted to figure out how to combine
convenience with Organic fair trade south american blends. Hand picked by workers and free from
artificial fertilizers and Pesticides. He wanted to bring real coffee to the people, not people to the coffee.

 

Today Bean Around the Block operates out of a mobile coffee truck, driving to various locations
throughout Greater Vancouver every day of the week. Our aim is to provide services where coffee
shops aren't conveniently located such as construction sites and recreational areas. Providing a
high quality product every time, everywhere!

 

[ Our Values ]

Quality

Quality coffee and quality service. Bean Around the Block believes in using only the
highest quality organic fair trade coffee product blends from South America allowing
us to serve our customers with a smile knowing be offer the best. The goal of our
team is to make every customer's day a little brighter and healthier, one cup at a time.

 

Convenience

The roots of Bean Around the Block stem from the need for convenience. Our purpose
is to add convenience to the lives of our customers by coming them.

 

Community

Giving back to the community is an integral part of what we do. We are part of a
community and we feel responsible for bringing the community together in positive
ways.

 

Environment

Caring for the environment is one of our highest priorities and we encourage our
customers and employees to adopt the same philosophy. We do everything that we
can do reduce our carbon footprint and have recently introduced 90% recycled
paper cups. Our coffee blends are certified fair trade and organic, we do this because
we feel all natural is the best for us and the environment together.
